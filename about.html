About Us

Meet the team behind Gems Taiwan — students, educators, and advocates for science literacy.

Our Mission

Gems Taiwan is a student-led education outreach organisation dedicated to bringing synthetic biology into Taiwan's classrooms. We believe science education should go beyond textbooks — sparking curiosity, critical thinking, and a sense of responsibility toward the world we are designing.
