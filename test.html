Science & Ethics

What is Synthetic Biology?

Synthetic biology combines biology, engineering and computational science to design and build novel biological systems.

Applications include drug development, biofuels, environmental remediation, and food technology.

Ethics of Gene Editing

We pursue responsible science communication and examine these ethical considerations:

  • Safety - Ensure safe and controlled use of technology
  • Equity - Ensure benefits are accessible to all
  • Environmental Responsibility - Consider ecological impact
  • Informed Consent - Promote transparency and participation
  • Respect for Cultural Values - Embrace diverse perspectives

Our Commitment

We are committed to responsible science education.

We emphasize ethical reflection, environmental awareness, and critical thinking as core educational values.
